You will now vote on those of the policy proposals that you just supported or disagreed with. You can now allocate multiple votes for the same issue under the Quadratic Voting mechanism

Important: You buy these votes with "vote-credits" (§). The cost in vote-credits for voting on an issue is the square of the number of votes you want to cast on that issue.

Example: If you want to vote 3 times for/against the minimum wage, you spend 9 credits on that issue.
